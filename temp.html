About Liloan, Cebu

Historical Landmarks | Beautiful Coastal Waters | Warm Community

Liloan is a scenic town located in the northern part of Cebu, Philippines. Known for its historical landmarks, natural beauty, and warm coastal waters, Liloan offers a tranquil escape for travelers seeking adventure, culture, and relaxation. Whether you're visiting the iconic Liloan Lighthouse, exploring serene beaches, or enjoying the rich culture, Liloan is a place where history and nature come together beautifully.

The town's rich heritage, combined with its picturesque surroundings, makes it an ideal spot for both locals and tourists. From vibrant community events to peaceful, quiet spots, Liloan offers a perfect mix of tradition and modernity.

Food and Culture

Liloan, Cebu is not only known for its scenic landscapes and historical landmarks but also for its rich culinary heritage. The town is home to a variety of delicious local delicacies that reflect its culture and traditions. From the chewy and sweet Masi, the crispy Otap, and crunchy Patatas, to the iconic Rosquillos, Liloan offers a delightful taste of its culture in every bite. These treats, often sold by local vendors, have become an essential part of the town's identity, making it a must-visit destination for food lovers.

Masi

When your appetite yearns for something chewy and sweet, Liloan's Masi is the answer. This homemade pastry made of glutinous rice and filled with sweet peanut paste is sold by vendors around the town's plaza and along the highway.

Otap

Otap is an oval-shaped puff pastry, crumpled and sprinkled with sugar. Fragile and easily broken, it offers a signature experience for lovers of sweets.

Rosquillos

Known as the delicacy Liloan is famous for, Rosquillos was created by Margarita "Titay" T. Frasco in 1907. With its crunchy exterior and sweet flavor, it complements various ways of enjoying it, such as dipping in a hot beverage or wrapping it around your fingers.

Patatas

A crunchy pastry that evokes childhood memories. A good alternative to salty corn or potato chips, Patatas are a nostalgic snack loved by many.
