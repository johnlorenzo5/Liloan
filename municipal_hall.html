ABOUT THE PLACE

The Liloan Municipal Hall is the central government building of Liloan, Cebu, located approximately 18 kilometers north of Cebu City. This hall serves as the administrative hub of the municipality, housing the offices of the mayor, vice mayor, and other local government officials. The building often features traditional Filipino architectural elements with a modern touch, reflecting the town's rich cultural heritage. It's situated near other key landmarks, including the Liloan Church (San Fernando Rey Parish) and the famous Bagacay Point Lighthouse (Parola), which is a symbol of the town.
