Events and Festivals

Discover the vibrant and colorful events and festivals celebrated in Liloan, Cebu.

San Fernando Rey Feast Day

Held in May, this religious celebration features a fluvial procession and community gatherings in honor of the town’s patron saint.

Rosquillos Festival

Celebrated every May, this festival honors the famous Liloan delicacy, rosquillos, with street dancing, parades, and food exhibits.

Christmas Festival of Lights

Every December, the town is lit up with festive decorations and events, including a giant Christmas tree lighting ceremony.

Barangay Fiesta Celebrations

Various barangays host their own fiestas throughout the year, showcasing unique traditions and local delicacies.
