About Liloan

Liloan is a scenic town located in the northern part of Cebu, Philippines. Known for its historical landmarks, natural beauty, and warm coastal waters, Liloan offers a tranquil escape for travelers seeking adventure, culture, and relaxation. From the majestic Liloan Lighthouse to serene beaches, there's something for everyone.

Liloan Lighthouse
