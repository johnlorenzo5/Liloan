TITAY'S LILOAN ROSQUILLOS

Liloan is renowned for its iconic delicacy, rosquillos, ring-shaped cookies created by Margarita "Titay" Frasco on April 3, 1907. Titay's culinary innovation became a household name, celebrated through a dedicated festival. Titay's Liloan Rosquillos, Inc., established in 1907, continues to uphold its legacy of exceptional baking.

LILOAN MUNICIPAL HALL

The Liloan Municipal Hall, situated around 18 kilometers north of Cebu City, serves as the main administrative center of Liloan, Cebu. It houses the offices of the mayor, vice mayor, and other key municipal officials, functioning as the heart of the local government.

STONE ARC

In Barangay Mulao, Liloan, a unique rock formation resembling Noah's Ark stands out with its arch-like structure supported by two rocks. Surrounded by various-sized stones, this natural attraction appeals to adventurers who enjoy stone-hopping treks. The Municipality of Liloan promotes it as a tourist destination, emphasizing safety precautions for those unfit for trekking or wary of cliffs.

LILOAN GOLF COURSE

Liloan, Cebu, is home to the Liloan Golf and Leisure Estate, a scenic golf course located within the town's lush and rolling landscapes. This golf estate offers an 18-hole course designed to provide both a relaxing and challenging experience for golf enthusiasts.
